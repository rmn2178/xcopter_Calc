# Safety Disclaimer

xcopterCalc is an estimation tool.

## Critical Notice
- All outputs are calculated estimates and may differ from real hardware behavior.
- Do not fly based only on calculator outputs.
- Always validate with bench tests, thermal checks, current measurements, and safe test procedures.
- Keep every component inside manufacturer limits (voltage, current, power, temperature, RPM).

## Liability
- This software is provided without warranty.
- Operators are fully responsible for verification, configuration, and flight safety.
- The project maintainers are not liable for direct or indirect damages caused by usage.

## Minimum Pre-Flight Validation
- Verify motor/ESC/battery ratings against expected max current and power.
- Verify propeller RPM against safe operating range.
- Validate hover current and thermal behavior with measured data.
- Confirm pack sag and remaining safety margin under load.
